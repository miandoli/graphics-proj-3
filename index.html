Controls (ON/OFF):

X: Translate continuously in the positive X direction.

C: Translate continuously in the negative X direction.

Y: Translate continuously in the positive Y direction.

U: Translate continuously in the negative Y direction.

Z: Translate continuously in the positive Z direction.

A: Translate continuously in the negative Z direction.

R: Rotate continuously around the X axis.

B: Pulsing the mesh faces continuously.
